Sentinel X

Started as a project, died as a legend

Around July 2020 (terrible year, ain't it) I started getting familiar with the programming language Python. wondered about how discord bots were created. I wanted to make 1 too. I just didn't know what exactly. I have been struggling to make anything of it. Bot names, Bot features, the ways how things were executed / updated. I was all new to that. 8-9 months (I took my time) learning Python 3, the API wrapper discord.py, figuring how to handle errors, etc etc. and here we are, Sentinel X at your service. keeping your discord members managed, managing channels, censoring / message deletion, auto moderation.

Few Words About Sentinel X

In the first period of the time Sentinel X had different names (a lot), just a few commands, It was just some experimenting. After around 8 months, I decided to try to take it to 75 Servers. Of course I hate advertizing just as you do. But just standing here, doing nothing won't get me any closer to my goals. My goals are to bring up a discord bot that would efficiently complete servers, helping server owners to manage their server, in a user-friendly way.
