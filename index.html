WHAT ARE THE FEATURES
WELCOME

Hello and welcome!

I'm a beginner developer and I want to offer quality for free. I hope you enjoy my discord bot. And please drop some suggestions in the suggestions discord channel of my discord server. It would only make Disghost better!

Why Disghost, out of countless other bots?

I aim to listen to my users what they want to have taken care of. Of course there are far better discord bots out there. But here, I listen to your requests, and there's a higher chance of implementing that request. I know lots of "feedback" systems where you. as a user, don't even get reply back or any kind of confirmation. My DMs are open, let the ideas flow!

Disghost

Started as a project, released as service

Around July 2020 (terrible year, ain't it) I started getting familiar with the programming language Python. wondered about how discord bots were created. I wanted to make 1 too. I just didn't know what exactly. I have been struggling to make anything of it. Bot names, Bot features, the ways how things were executed / updated. I was all new to that. 8-9 months (I took my time) learning Python 3, the API wrapper discord.py, figuring how to handle errors, etc etc. and here we are, Disghost at your service. keeping your discord members managed, managing channels, censoring / message deletion, auto moderation.

Few Words About Disghost

In the first period of the time Disghost had different names (a lot), just a few commands, It was just some experimenting. After around 8 months, I decided to try to take it to 75 Servers. Of course I hate advertizing just as you do. But just standing here, doing nothing won't get me any closer to my goals. My goals are to bring up a discord bot that would efficiently complete servers, helping server owners to manage their server, in a user-friendly way.
